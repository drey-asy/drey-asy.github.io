Proof

Published March 19, 2011 | By Drey

I received the print proof for The Stork the other day. It looks really good. It will look even better with the UV coating on the cover. There was a minor issue on one page (a thing strip of the image was in greyscale instead of color). Once that is resolved, the printer can do the full run.

Meanwhile, I am in the process of revamping my DreamPunk site and making a site just for The Stork. I’ve set up a Google map called “Stork Sightings” where you can see all the places The Stork has been.
